Apps

In the Retelling

The first app is In the Retelling, a missing word quote app published and supported by Considered Ideas Ltd.

The app draws from over 300 select quotes by more than 70 writers and thinkers from history, with one word hidden in each for the reader to complete. The app works fully offline. There are no accounts, no subscriptions, and no third-party trackers.
