01 In the Retelling

App

A daily quote with one missing word.

The first app is In the Retelling, a missing word quote app published and supported by Considered Ideas Ltd.

The app draws from over 300 select quotes by more than 70 writers and thinkers from history, with one word hidden in each for the reader to complete. The app works fully offline. There are no accounts, no subscriptions, and no third-party trackers.

02 HowManyAnagrams

App

A five-letter anagram puzzle.

A second app, HowManyAnagrams, is a five-letter anagram puzzle published and supported by Considered Ideas Ltd.

Each puzzle shows five letters and asks how many anagrams you can find, drawing from a finite, curated collection of more than 300 puzzles. The app works fully offline. There are no accounts, no subscriptions, and no third-party trackers.

03 Topsy Wordy

App

A six-letter word puzzle.

A third app, Topsy Wordy, is a six-letter word puzzle published and supported by Considered Ideas Ltd.

Each puzzle gives a jumble of letters. Find the word they make, then build it backwards. The app works fully offline. There are no accounts, no subscriptions, and no third-party trackers.
